Apple CarPlay Installation Services

What is Apple CarPlay?

Apple CarPlay connects your iPhone to your vehicle’s infotainment system, allowing you to access various apps and features, such as navigation, music, messaging, and more. With Apple CarPlay, you can:

  • Get directions with Apple Maps
  • Make and receive hands-free calls
  • Listen to music or podcasts via Apple Music
  • Send and receive messages using Siri

TWT Auto Care offers expert Apple CarPlay installation to bring the full functionality of your iPhone to your car, enhancing both safety and convenience. Android Auto Installation Services

What is Android Auto?

Android Auto offers similar features to Apple CarPlay, but is designed specifically for Android smartphone users. It connects your Android phone to your car’s infotainment system, providing access to apps like Google Maps, Spotify, Google Assistant, and more. With Android Auto, you can:

  • Get navigation and real-time traffic updates with Google Maps
  • Stream music and podcasts from Spotify, YouTube Music, and other apps
  • Send and receive messages using Google Assistant
  • Make calls hands-free

At TWT Auto Care, our technicians will install Android Auto with precision, ensuring your smartphone and car communicate flawlessly. With a simple and intuitive interface, Android Auto improves your driving experience while keeping you connected.
